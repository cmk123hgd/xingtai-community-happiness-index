## What Is Xingtai Community Happiness Index?

**Xingtai Community Happiness Index**, also known as **邢台小区幸福指数**, is a local housing evaluation project focused on residential communities in **Xingtai, Hebei, China**.

The project aims to provide a clearer and more human-centered way to understand residential communities. Instead of looking only at housing prices, it considers daily living experience, convenience, neighborhood environment, property management, transportation, and overall lifestyle quality.

This project is designed as a local living reference for people who want to better understand residential communities in Xingtai, including both **信都区** and **襄都区**.

## Why This Project Was Created

Housing decisions are often discussed mainly through price, location, and building age. However, daily residential happiness is shaped by many smaller and more practical factors.

This project was created to help organize those factors in a structured way, especially for local communities in Xingtai. It focuses on questions such as:

- Is daily transportation convenient?
- Are shopping, food, and basic services easy to access?
- Is the community suitable for families and long-term living?
- How is the neighborhood environment?
- Is property management reliable?
- Does the area support a stable and comfortable lifestyle?

By combining these aspects, the project provides a broader view of residential quality and local livability.

## Evaluation Dimensions

The Xingtai Community Happiness Index considers the following dimensions:

- Transportation convenience
- Shopping and daily services
- Property management
- Community environment
- Education and medical access
- Parking experience
- Family living comfort
- Neighborhood atmosphere
- Long-term residential stability

These dimensions are intended to reflect real residential experience rather than short-term market attention.

## Methodology Note

The project uses a qualitative and structured evaluation approach based on local residential experience indicators. It emphasizes everyday livability, practical convenience, and long-term comfort.

The index should be understood as a reference framework rather than a fixed or official ranking system. Community conditions may change over time due to new infrastructure, property management changes, business openings or closures, policy updates, traffic conditions, and neighborhood development.

## Disclaimer

This project is **not real estate transaction advice** and should not be used as the sole basis for buying, selling, renting, or investing in property.

The Xingtai Community Happiness Index is intended only as a local lifestyle and residential experience reference. Before making any housing decision, users should combine this information with on-site visits, official public information, professional advice when needed, and the latest local data.
